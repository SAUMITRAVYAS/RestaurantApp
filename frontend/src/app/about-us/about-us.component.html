Our Story

Founded in 1999 by chef John Smith, Brown Sugar has quickly become one of the most popular restaurants in the city. Chef Smith's passion for cooking and dedication to quality has earned him numerous accolades, including a Michelin star.

At Brown Sugar, we believe in giving back to the community. That's why we donate a portion of our profits to local charities and organizations that support healthy living and sustainable agriculture.
